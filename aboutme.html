About me

Hi, my name is Katarina and I’m currently studying to become a web designer and developer. One of the things I really enjoy is playing with colors and shapes, and then bringing them to life on the screen.
There’s something magical about seeing an idea transform into a living, interactive design.

If you’ve explored my navigation, you might have noticed that I also showcase my design and art — two things I’m truly proud of.
I’m a curious person who loves learning and constantly discovering new ways to express creativity.

Design is my greatest passion, and alongside web projects I also create digital art. For me, it’s not just about making something look beautiful, but also about telling a story and creating an experience that feels alive.
